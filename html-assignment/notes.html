HTML Tags

Five HTML tags with their description

Important text (<strong> </strong>):
Its a string of highly emphasized, much more important text. Screen readers will change their voice for this text.
Superscript text (<sup> </sup>):
It defines text as being subscript.
Line Break (<br>):
It creates a line break that’s significant to the content. Useful in poems and addresses where the division of lines is important.
Citation (<cite> </cite>):
Its a citation for another source, often used with quotations. A person’s name, a URL, a book, a movie title, etc.
Blockquote (<blockquote> </blockquote>):
Its a large, stand alone quote from another source.
